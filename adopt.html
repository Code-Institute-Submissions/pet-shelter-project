Adopt a dog

Are you looking to adopt a dog? That’s great news, of course, but don’t forget it’ll have to be an animal that’s a good fit with you and your lifestyle. Here at the Blackbrook pet shelter we’ll be only too happy to help. Our professionals know our animals like the back of their hand and they’ll help you find the perfect match. After all, making contented pets and happy owners is what we are all about. To have a look at all available dogs just click anywhere on this card and you’ll be taken to our dog gallery.

Adopt a cat

Are you looking to adopt a cat? We are happy to hear that! Our four-legged felines are looking forward to meeting you. What type of cat care you looking for though? Would you prefer a bundle of energy, or perhaps the feline equivalent of a couch potato? A young gun, or a cat that’s older and wiser? A cat that loves to be stroked, or an attention seeker? At the Blackbrook pet shelter we’ll be happy to find you a suited match. To have a look at all available cats just click anywhere on this card and you’ll be taken to our cat gallery.

All pets

Can't decide yet what type of four-legged critter you want to take home with you? No worries. At the Blackbrook pet shelter we have a wide variety of dogs and cats, most of them all different breeds and colours. Keep in mind that a cat or dog’s energy level can determine if your lifestyles will be compatible. Most dogs require 30 minutes to two hours of exercise per day, while cats can spend as much as 14 hours a day sleeping. To have a look at all available pets just click anywhere on this card and you’ll be taken to the gallery.
