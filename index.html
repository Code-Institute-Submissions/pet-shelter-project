Blackbrook pet shelter

At Blackbrook pet shelter we specialize in the rescue and rehoming of both dogs and cats. Upon arrival we take good care of them immediately and we try our best to find them suitable new owners. We have very experienced employees that provide lots of love and care to our animals. Our goal is to provide pets with a temporary safe place to stay until they get matched with a new best friend. Since 1992 we have succesfully found new loving homes for an estimated 2781 pets in the Blackbrook region. Whether you need to let go of a pet or you're interested in adopting, we've got you covered!

Adopting a pet

Are you looking for a new best friend? That is awesome news! But keep in mind that an animal has to fit with you and your lifestyle. Adopting a pet is a lifetime commitment that requires us to seriously consider our needs, lifestyle, and resources. We are eager to help you find a good match, just browse through our adoption page and fill in the contact form to set up a meeting.

Letting go of a pet

When unforseen circumstances have made it impossible for you to keep your pet, we are here for you. Good care will be provided for your pet until we find a suitable new owner. We understand that parting ways with your pet is a hard choice to make, but sometimes life circumstances leave us no choice. Thankfully we are here to help you out, visit the let go page for more information.
